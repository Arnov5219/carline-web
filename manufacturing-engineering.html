Manufacturing Engineering

We bridge the gap between initial product styling and final shopfloor execution. Our manufacturing engineering solutions focus on optimizing layout design, lowering cycle times, improving work ergonomics, and securing defect-free automated assembly.

01

Process Planning & Sequencing

Designing assembly operations step-by-step to maximize manufacturing efficiency, prevent production bottlenecks, and ensure logical tool layouts.

02

Assembly Line Simulation & FEA

Constructing 3D digital twins of cell structures to run line load balance analyses, coordinate system checks, and collision detection before mechanical build.

03

Precision Tool & Fixture Design

Designing heavy weld fixtures, ergonomic hand tools, modular grips, and automated robotic grippers tailored to structural sheet metal tolerances.

04

Shopfloor Work Instructions (SOPs)

Providing clear, detailed graphical Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), process control charts, and tooling instructions for quality assurance.
