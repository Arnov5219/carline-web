Automotive Services

Product Engineering

We provide end-to-end product engineering solutions that optimize performance, weight, noise control, safety, and manufacturability for global automotive manufacturers.

01

Body-in-White (BIW) Engineering

Conceptualizing and modeling body structures, including pillars, floor assemblies, roof bows, and body side panels, ensuring maximum torsional stiffness and lightweighting.

02

Interior & Exterior Trims

Developing injection-molded components such as bumpers, grills, dashboards, door cards, and pillar trim elements focusing on surface aesthetics and mechanical fit.

03

NVH & Acoustic Insulation

Designing engine bay shields, dashboard acoustic liners, and underbody dynamic dampers to suppress noise, vibration, and harshness (NVH) in passenger cabins.

04

Safety & Regulatory Analysis

Optimizing sheet metal deformation structures for energy absorption during collisions, meeting global FMVSS and Euro NCAP crash safety standards.
