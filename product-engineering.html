Product Engineering Workflow

Our structured workflow develops automotive concepts into production-ready CAD models, assemblies, and engineering documentation.

Stage 01

EXTERIOR/INTERIOR TRIMS/NVH PARTS AND BIW STRUCTURE /REINFORCEMENTS

A comprehensive Product Engineering service encompassing the elements of design specifications, benchmarks, concept make, final CAD and drawing Release and change management.

We support in Packaging, deciding assembly sequence, making master sections, Engineering design to meet customer requirements, managing variants in different layouts and different vehicle specifications.

We also support for DMU release through Team center.

Stage 02

PLANNING AND EXECUTION

We support Product Engineering in NX and CATIA software. Our team is focused and equipped with domain experience to provide engineering solutions throughout the product development lifecycle.

Stage 03

CONCEPT DESIGN

We support in making Master Sections, Initial packaging, basic concept with respect to Class-A surface.

Stage 04

FINAL DESIGN

We optimize the design with detail engineering. We check for packaging, compliance as per regulations depending on the customer requirement, manufacturability. We make CAD data with parametric Solid modelling. We support to release the final CAD data though team center.

Stage 05

2D DETAILING/ DRAWING RELEASE

Upon the design approval from the customer, we support for 2D detailing for Part/assembly.

Stage 06

ENGINEERING CHANGE MANAGEMENT

We support with design changes. We provide engineering solution through out the product development lifecycle.
